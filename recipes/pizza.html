Pizza

Pizza is a baked flatbread dish originating from Italy. It consists of a round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is baked at high heat until the crust is crisp and the cheese is fully melted.

Ingredients

    Base

  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

    • Sauce

  • Tomato puree
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Dried oregano

    • Toppings

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Onion
  • Capsicum
  • Olives

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220 °C.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
  4. Add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
