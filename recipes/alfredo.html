Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine Alfredo is a Western pasta dish made by coating long flat pasta in a rich cream-based sauce. The sauce is smooth, heavy, and binds tightly to the pasta, creating a uniform and filling dish.

Ingredients

    Pasta

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

    • Alfredo sauce

  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and add garlic.
  3. Add cream and simmer gently.
  4. Add parmesan and stir until thick.
  5. Add drained pasta and coat evenly.
  6. Serve hot.
